Frequently Asked Questions
Is MatricAce only for Grade 12 students? +
Not at all! MatricAce is built for Grade 10, 11 and 12 students. We firmly believe that matric preparation shouldn't start in Grade 12 — the earlier you build your foundation, the better you'll perform when it counts. Grade 10 and 11 students get access to CAPS-aligned study content across all major subjects, the same AI tools, and can access all Grade 12 materials too. One app, your whole high school journey.
Do I need a credit card to try Premium? +
No credit card is required to create an account or access the free tier. When upgrading to Premium, payment is processed securely via Flutterwave. Your 3-day free trial begins immediately upon account creation.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. You can manage and cancel your subscription at any time from your Profile page within the app. There are no cancellation fees and no lock-in periods.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major South African debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), as well as mobile money options — processed securely through Flutterwave.
Is MatricAce aligned to the CAPS curriculum? +
Yes. All content on MatricAce — including quizzes, notes, mock exams and AI explanations — is strictly aligned to the Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement (CAPS) for Grade 10, 11 and 12 learners.
Is my data safe? What about POPIA? +
Your data is protected in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We only collect what is necessary to provide the service, we never sell your data, and you can request deletion at any time. Full details are available in our Privacy Policy.
Do you offer school or group pricing? +
Yes! We offer promo codes for schools, tutors and educators. If you're a teacher wanting to bring MatricAce to your class, contact us at [email protected] and we'll set you up.

The Journey

How MatricAce came to be.

MatricAce started with a frustration shared by millions of South African students: too many generic study apps that don't understand the NSC, too few affordable tools that actually match how the DBE sets exams.

We set out to build something different. Something that speaks the language of South African education. Something that a student in a rural school in Limpopo can use just as effectively as one in a private school in Sandton.

Today, MatricAce covers 27 CAPS subjects across Grade 10, 11 and 12, has thousands of CAPS-aligned questions, and uses cutting-edge AI to provide personalised explanations — all for as little as R60 a month, with a generous free tier for those who can't afford even that.

🇿🇦 Meet the Founder

The Person Behind MatricAce

Abdullah Muhydeen
Founder & CEO

I'm Abdullah, the founder of MatricAce. I'm not originally from South Africa — I moved here in 2021 to study Information Technology, drawn by how highly recognised South African degrees are worldwide. Coming here was one of the best decisions I've ever made.

Shortly after arriving, I started reading about the matric system — the pressure, the pass rates, the stories of students who worked incredibly hard but didn't have the right resources to back them up. As an outsider looking in, it struck me how much potential was being left on the table. South African students are talented and driven, but the tools available to them didn't reflect that.

So I decided to build something. Not because I went through matric myself, but because I saw the gap clearly and had the skills to do something about it. I built MatricAce from scratch — all 27 NSC subjects, DBE-aligned mock exams, AI study plans — with one goal: to make sure no South African student — whether in Grade 10, 11 or 12 — ever has to feel underprepared for matric again. This country gave me an opportunity, and this is my way of giving something back.
