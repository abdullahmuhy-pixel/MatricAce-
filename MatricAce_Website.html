Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need a credit card to try Premium? +
No credit card is required to create an account or access the free tier. When upgrading to Premium, payment is processed securely via Flutterwave. Your 3-day free trial begins immediately upon account creation.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. You can manage and cancel your subscription at any time from your Profile page within the app. There are no cancellation fees and no lock-in periods.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major South African debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), as well as mobile money options — processed securely through Flutterwave.
Is MatricAce aligned to the CAPS curriculum? +
Yes. All content on MatricAce — including quizzes, notes, mock exams and AI explanations — is strictly aligned to the Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement (CAPS) for Grade 12 NSC learners.
Is my data safe? What about POPIA? +
Your data is protected in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We only collect what is necessary to provide the service, we never sell your data, and you can request deletion at any time. Full details are available in our Privacy Policy.
Do you offer school or group pricing? +
Yes! We offer promo codes for schools, tutors and educators. If you're a teacher wanting to bring MatricAce to your class, contact us at [email protected] and we'll set you up.

Our Story

Built for students
who refuse to fail.

MatricAce was born from a simple belief: every South African student deserves access to world-class study tools — regardless of their school or their budget. We built the app we wish existed when we were doing Matric.

🎯
Our Mission
To make quality Matric preparation accessible to every South African Grade 12 learner — from rural schools to urban academies — through AI-powered tools built specifically for the NSC curriculum.

The Journey

How MatricAce came to be.

MatricAce started with a frustration shared by millions of South African students: too many generic study apps that don't understand the NSC, too few affordable tools that actually match how the DBE sets exams.

We set out to build something different. Something that speaks the language of South African education. Something that a student in a rural school in Limpopo can use just as effectively as one in a private school in Sandton.

Today, MatricAce covers 27 NSC subjects, has thousands of CAPS-aligned questions, and uses cutting-edge AI to provide personalised explanations — all for as little as R30 a month, with a generous free tier for those who can't afford even that.
